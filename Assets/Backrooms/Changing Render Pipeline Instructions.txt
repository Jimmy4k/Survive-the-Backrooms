To change render pipeline to HRDP or URP, please do the following:

1.) Delete these folders and their contents 
- Materials
- Meshes
- Prefabs
- Scene
- Scripts
- Shaders
- Sound

(Do not Delete Textures Folder)

2.) Extract your chosen unitypackage patch file
Backrooms HDRP Patch
or 
Backrooms URP Patch
